About Us

Deto Shipyards Limited is a world-class producer of advanced boats for naval patrol, escort, attack, maritime interdiction, and border protection mission on the waterway. We also produce commercial boats for secondary market (leisure, fishing etc.,) whenever there is demand. Since its inception, Deto Shipyards Limited has built a reputation for high quality products, which help the user to perform their job effectively.
